Sales & Marketing

Is your company finding it difficult to fill sales & marketing jobs? Then, it is time you get in touch with Next Horizon Talent. As a specialized recruitment firm, we very well understand the difficulty in filling these vacancies. These jobs need special traits and qualifications to be fruitful and, our recruiters know how to make them so.

While the majority think sales & marketing are one and the same, the reality is different. Marketing comes first and, sales follow the former. In a strict sense, marketing is creating an interest in people to buy a product or a service. On the other hand, a sale consists of actually selling a product or a service. With this phenomenal difference, we know how hard it can be for employers to fill these positions.
